Got Wood Floors

Web Design, Logo

Visit Site

Overview

Got Wood Floors is a new start-up wood flooring company dedicated to using only real wood for all its services. Whether you need floor installation or repairs, they are ready to help you with 25 years of experience under their belt.

Design

This was my first paid job as a freelance web developer, thus I consulted heavily with my client. The client wanted a simple yet informative website that was easy on the eyes. With that in mind, I developed a simple site with all the info on a single scrollable page. The client also wanted a simple and small logo as well, so I also drew up and created the "GW" logo.

Other Details

  • Developed in 2017
  • Created using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Foundation.

Designing Services

To make a simple, but informative look, I added pictures with services to show the visitor what services Got Wood Floors provides. Just using pictures to show the services offer a clean and minimalistic view. If the visitor needs more information of what a certain kind of service is, they can click on the the service to get an in-depth description.
