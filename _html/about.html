ABOUT ME

I like to make ideas into reality! I'll take your vision and display them on the web. Whether the project is small or big, you'll find that I'm a hard worker who desires to put out my best effort each and everytime. Where others may see problems, I see challenges that will be solved. I welcome them because it'll make the end product that much better for you and your clients.

IDEAS

I collaborate with my clients to help our ideas grow into an ideal and well-planned designs. My goal is to make sure clients are satisfied and proud of their design.

DEVELOPMENT

I plan out the structure of the website, focusing on responsive web design, intuitive UI, and creative interactions fit for the client's needs. I keep in mind that users often leave web pages that are unintuitive. To make them stay longer I develop the site to flow and have a clear design. I strive to create memorable interactions for the users to help your site grow.

CODE

I design with multiple browsers -- Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge -- in mind on multiple platforms -- PC, Mac, tablets, phones. Generally, I code from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript using front-end frameworks like Foundation and Bootstrap, but I also use Wordpress. I also have expertise in programming for back-end using PHP and MySQL.
